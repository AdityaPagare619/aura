1. Executive Summary
AURA v4 is an ambitious on-device AI assistant implemented as a Rust daemon loaded via JNI into an Android app, distributed through Termux. The architecture is fundamentally sound — the JNI bridge is well-structured, the physics-based power/thermal models are impressive, and the OEM-aware doze management shows real-world Android experience.

The accessibility service has node recycling bugs that will leak memory on every screen read.
Overall Grade: B- — Strong architecture, needs hardening before field deployment.
